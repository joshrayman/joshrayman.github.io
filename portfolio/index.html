Jump to project:

2018 Afghan election

Data analysis, explainer content and results dashboard

Interactive data dashboard for pre-election coverage, and updated with results as they arrived. Adapted into a touchscreen TV segment that was broadcast on BBC Persian and BBC Pashto. Created original data analysis of the 2010 and 2018 elections, and built graphics explaining the electoral system.

Article: How We Visualized an Election with No Political Parties (OpenNews Source)

Role: data, design, development

2018 Turkish election

Interactive explainer and results dashboard

A series of interactive pieces for the Turkish service before and after the snap Presidential/Parliamentary elections in June 2018. Included: a results dashboard in the week after the election, an interactive explainer of the D'Hondt seat distribution system, a bite-sized guide to party manifestos, and a hex map of the 2018 results.

Role: research, data, development

Explore the IS Tunnels

3D Model/UX Project

An explorable 3D model scanned from an excavated tunnel. User interface was built over the top of a third party 3D tool, and we enhanced how the camera movement worked.
